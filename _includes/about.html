About

My journey is supported by an educational foundation in Advertising and Marketing Communications Management and Business Administration, along with Autodesk certifications in CAD/CAM, preparing me for new challenges in this technical field.

My passion for automobiles and making has fueled a mechanically intuitive mindset and a self-taught skillset in CAD, 3D printing, CNC routing, and the mechanical assembly and inspection of devices like automotive engines, transmissions and differentials. This hands-on experience in automotive technology reinforces my adeptness with complex systems, aligning with my career transition toward mechanical technology and quality control roles.
